How we are working?

Each stage is thought out to the smallest detail.

Cost
calculation
With the help of the designer, you can choose a thoughtful style and a complete set of the interior or independently choose everything to your taste. You see whether additional payments for individual items will be required, as well as the final amount of the repair. After calculating the cost, we do not increase the estimate in the production process.
Getting started
and workflow
We prepare the necessary documents and sign a repair contract with you, which strictly fixes the cost and deadlines – these parameters will not be changed in the future.
Draft and
engineering work
Further, the specialists of our company begin rough work, which takes up most of the repair time — 25-55 days. The level of rough work is the foundation that guarantees the durability and quality of repairs.
After
roughing work
Then, after the completion of the stage of roughing and engineering work, we perform all the work in the bathroom and toilet, lay the flooring, prepare the walls for painting. Toward the end of the repair, we install plumbing, doors and carry out painting work. All operations take from one to two weeks.
